My development process in designing

In the past, I developed a passion for art and design during my high school years. I enjoyed sketching, experimenting with colors, and creating visually appealing compositions. This early interest in creativity sparked my desire to become a graphic designer.

I decided to pursue my dream by enrolling in Computer Science Course at a local college because that is the only available course that is related to designing. I learned the fundamentals of computer so i can be familiarize with the softwares and tools in digital design, I also gained proficiency in design software such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

I plan to continue building my portfolio with a diverse range of projects, pushing the boundaries of my creativity, and exploring new design techniques and styles. Additionally, I'm considering further education or certifications in specialized areas of design to expand my skill set and stay competitive in the field.
